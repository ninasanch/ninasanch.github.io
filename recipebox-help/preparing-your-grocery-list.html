Preparing Your Grocery List

On this page

  • Adding ingredients to your grocery list
  • Printing your grocery list

Adding ingredients to your grocery list

  1. Tap on the recipe you would like to add to your grocery list.
  2. Tap Save on the top right.
  3. Tap Add To List under the serving size.
  4. Tap the Select All in the top right.
    Alternatively: Tap the specific ingredients you would like to add, tap Add when it appears in the top right.
  5. To view your grocery list, go to bottom toolbar and tap the My List.

Printing your grocery list

  1. Tap My List in the bottom toolbar.
  2. To delete any ingredients from the list, press and hold the ingredient, tap Delete when it appears.
  3. Scroll the grocery list to ensure you are satisfied with the list.
  4. Tap the Printer Icon in the top right.
  5. Change any specific printer settings you may have.
  6. Tap Print in the top right.
