Organizing Recipes

On this page

  • Creating a category for a recipe
  • Adding a category to a recipe
  • Favoriting a recipe

Creating a category for a recipe

  1. Open the RecipeBox app and go to My Recipes.
  2. Select the recipe you would like to categorize.
  3. At the top of the page, tap on the Ellipsis Icon, and select Edit Categories in the drop down menu.
  4. Enter the name for a new category in the text box labeled Add a new Category and press enter.
  5. Your new category will now be saved under the selected recipe.

Adding a category to a recipe

  1. Open the RecipeBox app and go to My Recipes.
  2. Select the recipe you would like to add an existing category to.
  3. At the top of the page, tap on the Ellipsis Icon, and select Edit Categories in the drop down menu.
  4. Choose the existing category you would like to add in the Other Categories menu.
  5. Your chosen category will now be added to the recipe.

Favoriting a recipe

  1. Open the RecipeBox app and go to My Recipes.
  2. Select the recipe you would like to favorite.
  3. At the top of the page, tap on the Heart Icon to favorite the recipe.
