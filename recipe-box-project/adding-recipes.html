Adding Recipes

On this page

  • Discovering recipes using the search bar
  • Discovering recipes by browsing
  • Saving recipes from the app
  • Adding a recipe from a URL
  • Adding a custom recipe
  • Editing a recipe in your list
  • Deleting a recipe from your list

Discovering recipes using the search bar

  1. Go to Discovery.
  2. Tap the search bar.
  3. Type a keyword relating to the recipe you want to find.
  4. Tap Search.
  5. Tap the recipe you're interested in.

Discovering recipes by browsing

  1. Go to Discovery.
  2. Scroll down the screen to browse recipes.
  3. Tap the recipe you're interested in.

Saving recipes from the app

  1. Open the recipe.
  2. Tap Save on the top right of the screen.

Adding a recipe from a URL

  1. Go to Add Recipe.
  2. Under Copy and Paste URL, paste the recipe's URL in the text box.
  3. Tap Save URL.

Adding a custom recipe

  1. Go to Add Recipe.
  2. Under Create Your Own Recipe, tap Add Recipe.
  3. Type the recipe information (Title, ingredients, instructions, nutrition, etc.).
  4. Tap Save on the top right of the screen.

Editing a recipe in your list

  1. Go to My Recipes.
  2. Tap on the recipe you want to edit.
  3. Tap on the Ellipsis Icon on the top right of the screen.
  4. Tap Edit Recipe.
  5. Scroll to the textbox you want to edit, and make the changes.
  6. Tap Save on the top right of the screen.

Deleting a recipe from your list

  1. Go to My Recipes.
  2. Tap on the recipe you want to edit.
  3. Tap on the Ellipsis Icon on the top right of the screen.
  4. Tap Delete Recipe.
  5. On the confirmation window, tap Delete Recipe.
