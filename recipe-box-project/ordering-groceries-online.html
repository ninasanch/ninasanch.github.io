Ordering Groceries Online

On this page

  • Changing your grocery retailer
  • Ordering your groceries

Changing your grocery retailer

  1. Go to Change Grocery Retailer.
  2. Type your U.S.A. ZIP code.
  3. Tap Start Search.
  4. Tap on the retailer you want to use.
  5. Log in with your account.

Ordering your groceries

  1. Go to My List.
  2. Tap Shop This List Online.
  3. For each item on your grocery list, scroll through the retailer web page, and add the item to your cart. Tap Next.
  4. Tap Checkout when you're done.
  5. Enter the pickup/delivery details and payment details.
