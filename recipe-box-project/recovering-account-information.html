Recovering Account Information

  • Recovering your username
  • Resetting your password

Recovering your username

  1. Visit the forgot username page to recover your username.
  2. Enter the email address for your account.
  3. Select Send Me My Username.
  4. If you are a registered RecipeBox user, your username will be sent to your email inbox.

Resetting your password

  1. Visit the forgot password page to reset your password.
  2. Enter the email address for your account.
  3. Select Reset Password.
  4. Check your email inbox for a link to reset your password. Follow the instructions on the linked page.
