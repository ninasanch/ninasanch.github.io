Sharing Recipes

On this page

  • Printing your recipe
  • Sharing your recipe digitally

Printing your recipe

  1. Tap My Recipes in the bottom toolbar.
  2. Tap on the recipe that you would like to print.
  3. Tap on the Ellipsis Icon in the top right.
  4. Tap Print Recipe.
  5. Change any specific printer settings you may have.
  6. Tap Print in the top right.

Sharing your recipe digitally

  1. Tap My Recipes in the bottom toolbar.
  2. Tap on the recipe that you would like to share.
  3. Tap the Share Icon in the top right.
  4. Tap Copy.
  5. Open your messaging app (Ex: Gmail, iMessage, Text message, WhatsApp, etc.).
  6. Start a message to your recipient.
  7. Paste your recipe link.
  8. Type any additional details in your message.
  9. Send the message.
